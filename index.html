Welcome to ACE College & Kiddies

ACE College & Kiddies is a prestigious government approved institution located in Ibadan, Nigeria, dedicated to nurturing future leaders from the cradle to secondary school. The name ACE, which stands for Academy Center for Excellence, reflects our unwavering commitment to providing top-quality education rooted in strong moral values. Our guiding slogan, "Excellent with Integrity," defines the core of our philosophy.
