    ACE Model College & Kiddies

Welcome to ACE Model College & Kiddies

ACE Model College & Kiddies is a prestigious government approved institution located in Ibadan, Nigeria, dedicated to nurturing future leaders from the cradle to secondary school. The name ACE, which stands for Academy Center for Excellence, reflects our unwavering commitment to providing top-quality education rooted in strong moral values. Our guiding slogan, “Excellent with Integrity,” defines the core of our philosophy.

Our Vision

To be a beacon of academic and moral excellence, raising children who lead with knowledge, confidence, and character.

Our Mission

Our mission is to offer a nurturing and academically rich environment where children are empowered to reach their full potential from the earliest stages of development through to graduation.

Our Core Values

Integrity, Excellence, Creativity, and Service

The Kiddies Section Crèche, Playgroup, Nursery & Primary

At ACE Kiddies, we believe that every child deserves a solid foundation. Our Crèche and Playgroup programs provide a warm, safe, and engaging space where young minds are introduced to early learning through structured play and interaction. Our Nursery and Primary School foster curiosity, creativity, and academic readiness. With a blend of modern teaching methods and traditional values, we help pupils build strong literacy, numeracy, and problem-solving skills, while also developing confidence and moral responsibility.

ACE Model College Secondary Education

The secondary arm of our school ACE Model College is known for its pursuit of academic distinction, character formation, and leadership training. We prepare students for both national and international examinations through a dynamic curriculum and hands-on learning experiences. Beyond the classroom, students participate in excursions, debates, science fairs, and other co-curricular activities. These experiences like our trip to Ilaji Resorts expand their worldviews and instill teamwork, discipline, and innovation.

Dedicated Educators

Our teachers are not just educators; they are mentors who guide and inspire our students. They are trained to create a supportive and enriching environment that fosters growth and confidence.

Our Core Values

Community Service

We instill a sense of responsibility in our students. They learn to give back to their community through regular service projects and community outreach programs.
